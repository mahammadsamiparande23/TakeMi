user_role.txt


1. Admin(me):
	- Manage all users.
	- Approve or reject vendor (Shopkeeper).
	- view all users.
	- Manage platform settings.

2. Vendor (Shopkeepers or Sellers):
	
	- Add and edit products.
	- Manage inventory.
	- View and fulfill their orders.
	- View Earning

3. Buyer (customer):
	- Browse products.
	- Place orders.
	- track Orders.
	- Leave reviews
